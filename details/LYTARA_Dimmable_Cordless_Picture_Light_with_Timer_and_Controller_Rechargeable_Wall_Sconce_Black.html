LYTARA Dimmable Cordless Picture Light

Elevate your artistic space with the LYTARA Dimmable Cordless Picture Light. This modern and versatile lighting solution brings elegance and convenience to any wall display, whether it's a gallery, living room, or bedroom setup.

Enhance Your Art With Precision Lighting

Featuring 70 high-quality LED beads, this cordless light ensures even and broad illumination that highlights the colors, textures, and details of your artwork, paintings, portraits, or other wall decor. The dimming functionality allows you to customize the brightness to suit any mood or setting, while the 3 lighting modes offer different color temperatures (3000K, 4000K, 6500K) for a wide range of visual effects.

Convenient and Eco-Friendly Design

Equipped with a built-in rechargeable 5000mAh battery, this light eliminates the need for frequent battery changes and offers a hassle-free power solution. The Type-C charging system ensures quick and easy recharging, making it perfect for everyday use. Its cordless design allows for flexible placement, making it ideal for creative or temporary displays.

Control at Your Fingertips

The IR remote control lets you conveniently adjust brightness, color temperature, and set timers from anywhere in the room. Whether you're setting up a display for a party or a quiet evening, you can easily automate your lighting schedule and create the perfect ambiance. The adjustable pivot bracket offers optimal light direction and control over shadow effects, ensuring your artwork is always beautifully lit.

Sleek and Stylish

With its modern black metal finish, the LYTARA Cordless Picture Light is a stylish addition to any interior. Its minimalist design blends seamlessly with contemporary decor, making it both functional and aesthetically pleasing. The magnetic LED light bar can be rotated and repositioned effortlessly, allowing for endless lighting possibilities and a personalized look.

Whether you're showcasing art, photographs, or even a dartboard, this light ensures your items are always beautifully highlighted.
